    Tutor Support Sessions HB

    The course offers a facility to book 20-minute, 1-to-1 support calls with one of our tutors. This is aimed at allowing students with particular queries to have these answered and walked-through, in a personalised setting.

    As an example, these queries can either be about a subject area, assignment question, or Final Project.

    Booking a Session

    1. Visit the lesson resources home page: this is the first link under the 'Lesson Resources' heading, in your repository's ReadMe

    2. Click the '1-to-1 Support' button, and select a tutor from the list.

    3. From the listed availability, select a slot that suits you, and proceed with making a booking

    4. Please add the query or full reason for the call into the description field

    5. Make the booking.
    You should receive an email notification, and if you synchronise your calendar with the booking utility, an event will be added into your calendar for you to accept, automatically.

    At 1:41 of the linked video, we briefly walk you through where to find the 1-to-1 booking links.

    Attending Your Session

    We would like to remind you to mark and accept 1-to-1 calendar invites, so that you do not miss your session. Our tutors are often under high demand, and so we encourage those with bookings to keep them, or let us know, in advance, by email if they need to cancel or rearrange. For any such queries or questions about this support resource, please contact us at hello@scryptiq.ai.
